Jack Black at an Eyebrow Furrow:

  • He shares the same birthday as Jason Priestly
  • Both of his parents were pretty cool scientists: His mom Judith Love Cohen was an Aerospace engineer who worked on both the Hubble Space telescope and the Apollo space missions. His dad Neil Siegal basically invented mobile GPS devices.
  • He met his wife Tanya Haden in high school. Tanya is not only a triplet is also a professional Cellist.
  • Because of his appearance in a parody of the Council of Elrond during the 2002 MTV Movie Awards, Peter Jackson cast him in King Kong in 2005.
